2. Literature Review on The Impact of UI Design Quality on Shopper Behavior (Gabriel Evan Gunawan)

In the era of e-commerce shopping, the user interface (UI) plays a crucial role in shaping shoppers' online experiences and influencing their purchasing decisions. This literature review examines the effect of UI design quality on shoppers, exploring the relationship between UI elements, user experience, and consumer behavior.
According to HW Yoon (2020), Intuitive navigation systems and well-structured information architecture contribute to shoppers' ease of finding products, reducing cognitive load, and increasing their likelihood of completing a purchase. Clear and concise labeling, categorization, and search functionalities play a critical role in enhancing the browsing and decision-making process. Also, based on research done by Jung. W(2017), UI designs that incorporate personalized product recommendations based on user preferences, browsing history, and demographic information enhance shopper engagement, increase conversion rates, and drive repeat purchases.
Next, according to Fu. J(2022), The responsiveness of a UI, including page loading speed, smooth scrolling, and quick feedback, significantly impacts user engagement. Slow-loading pages and unresponsive UI elements will frustrate shoppers and can lead to abandonment of a shopping process.
Finally, according to research from D.Schubert(2016), as mobile commerce continues to grow, UI designs that can adapt to mobile devices are crucial. Mobile-friendly interfaces with responsive layouts and simplified interactions improve shopper satisfaction and support repeat purchases. Furthermore, the way products are presented through UI elements, such as high-resolution images, zoom features, and detailed descriptions, affects shoppers' perception of the product's value and influences purchase intent.
In conclusion, this literature review explains the significant influence of UI design quality on shoppers and their behavior. A well-designed UI that incorporates visual appeal, intuitive navigation, usability, persuasive elements, and personalization can impact shopper engagement, satisfaction, and repeat purchase. As online shopping continues to evolve, businesses must recognize the importance of investing in UI design to create exceptional shopper experiences and gain a competitive advantage in the digital marketplace.
